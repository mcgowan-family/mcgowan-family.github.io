Feb 08

Been a while since last update. Back in last summer, it was still me and Sarah cutting about together. We headed out to West Wales in August and went to Ramsay Island (saw the first seal pups of the year). For some reason Sarah decided to take me up Crickley Hill and cook a barbeque - it was great :o) And Francis took us round to see his new house. Sure, it'll be great and we'll be in by Christmas ;o)

Headed over to Spain in October and caught up with most of family.

For some reason had a pudding party! Lots of friends came along, and the winner was ....... New York cheesecake!

Leo threw a bonfire party and Steve let his hair down.

Headed over to Washington with work in November. Some folk hadn't done the DC tour, so we took in most of the sites.

December was a big month. Patrick William was born at 0947 on 11 December. He is a great little man and he's been very busy since he got here.

cheers, Paul
