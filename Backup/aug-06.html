' Right Here

August 06

The month started of with a quick trip up to Snowdon with Raph to get our climbing skills back up for Old Man of Stoer in September. We stayed in the bunkhouse at Capel Curig and climbed at Idwal Slabs and Minas Mot in Llanberis. The climbing in Llanberis was followed by a bit of gratuitous skinny dipping in the Afon Nant Peris. Fortunately no images of this can be brought to you at this time. I finally got to Pete's Eats in Llanberis, which is definitely more famous for its history than its breakfasts :o)

Popped down to London for a great weekend at Toby's. As ever, London was a fabulous mix of unrelated images and highlights :o) The London parks were parched. This Rangers supporter had just heard the Dunfermline score. These lads were celebrating the fact that there would be another season of Love Island. I was sleeping just up the road from 22 suspected terrorists in Paddington Green station. The V&A was the V&A. And I finallly got to a show at The Globe (Coriolanus doncha know) which was fabulous, if a little damp, in parts :o)

Finished of the month up in Scotland at Geraldine and Etienne's wedding. More pic's here. Got some biking in as well at the Pedal for Scotland event, along with a number of other rides out with Michael (including the Wednesday night bash with the Glasgow MTB club). Great fun riding up and down the M6 with a full rack.

cheers, Paul
