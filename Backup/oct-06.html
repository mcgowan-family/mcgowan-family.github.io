' Right Here

September/October 06

September didn't pan out as expected. Plan was for the Assynt Expedition in Second week, then off to Vietnam for next 2 weeks. First bit went as planned, but Dad died at end of that week so it all went a bit pear-shaped for a while.

I'll come back to Assynt in a minute, but there's no avoiding we were all pretty shook up by Dad's death. It's something to do with losing the whole generation. But no regrets and no complaints; his was a life well lived and he left us all wanting more. Not a bad way to go.

The Assynt trip was incredible. It has to be one of the more successful trips in terms of hitting the targets and just outrageous road trip big adventure fun.

I've lost the plot on October. The Challenger ball was cool. Shell came down, we parted the night away at the Daffodil then ended up with Chris, Stu, Pia and the remnants of the band in SubTone.

cheers, Paul
