Winter 09 - 10

The big news since the last update has been the arrival of Bryher into the family. More on that later.

Knowing that Bryher was on the way, we wanted to make sure that Patrick had a few special days to remember when it was just us and him:

  • And we went to Slimbridge for the first time and found WellyBoot Land!

    • Sarah's brother Rob got married to Jackie in October. Patrick was well turned out and popped up in a few shots. This is his first suit. Freya looked after him all day. And we had a very nice time as well :o)

    And then at the end of October, at 1636 on the 27th, baby Bryher weighed in at 11 lb 1 oz. Very healthy :o) Mother and baby were very well. Dad was taking it all in.

    Auntie Caroline got her arms round the posse a week later.

    Patrick took a wee while to get used to not being the centre of everything. We went for a nice walk up Timbercombe Lane and took him down to Magic Land in Cirencester where he got the hang of the cannons pretty quick. And then it was time for his 2nd Birthday!

    Pretty soon it was Christmas. Someone was very curious on Christmas Eve. And by the time the big day arrived, he was just blown away.
