September 05

Been in USA for most of September. Been hiking & climbing in High Sierras of California with Fitz for first half of month. Now in San Francisco and heading over to East Coast tonite.

Spent first week in Yosemite. Did some climbing and some great long hikes and back-country camping. Didnt see any bears, but just about everything else.

Drove round through Tuolumne Meadows and over Tioga Pass down into Owens Valley for second weeks climbing. Did Mount Whitney and Mount Muir. Met some great folk and would have sumitted on Mount Russell had we not got snowed off.

Latter part of trip spent on Pacific Coast. Hit the coast around Morro Bay and drove up to SF, via Monterey. Spent last couple of days in San Fran enjoying the hospitality of Paul and Eunice who were good enough to let us crash. Saw a wilder side of SF than previously!

Will spend next week working on East Coast then back to UK.

cheers, Paul
