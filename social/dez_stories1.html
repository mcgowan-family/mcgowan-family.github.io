Dezmond stayed with me in London a few times. On one visit, we were out on the town and came across a publicity stunt for the latest Sylvester Stallone movie. There was a motorbike that featured as a central prop in the film - all chrome and over the top gadgets. This bike was being displayed in a shop window and there was a big crowd standing round the window checking it out.

The area was roped off to stop the crowd getting too close. Me and Dez were standing watching all this going on. One minute, I was standing next to Dez looking at the bike, and the next minute I was standing watching Dez sitting on the bike, in the shop window, with this huge grin on his face.

Security guards appeared from all over and started dragging Dez off the bike and he just hung on, grinning. That's what he was like.

Steve MacMurray
