Chris and Ashliegh's Wedding, 23rd February, Fortingall

In mid Feb 2016, I drove up to Scotland, picked up my brother Brian and headed into . The date for Ashliegh and Chris's wedding had been set many years before, the venue at Fortingall at the mouth of Glen Lyon was a more recent addition - but what a great venue!

Me and Brian had headed up to the day before to the high pass at the head of the glen to mess around in the snow - there is a road under this. But the next day, it was time to get into your highland gear and wait for the bride to arrive.

Fortingall Vilage Hall was an inspired location and was the perfect backdrop to the main event.

Maureen seldom looked better. Not sure if it's Gerry's best shot, but pretty funny. Caroline and John looking well. They'll always have each other :)

It really was one of the loveliest weddings ever.
