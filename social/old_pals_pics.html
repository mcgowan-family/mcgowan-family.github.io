Pals pic's from the 80's.

  • Bivvying on Achmelvich beach, 83, with Bruno, Fitz, Fike and Ge.
  • Just cause we could, me and Fitz walked from Milngavie to Fort William. It was hard work, so we cooled our feet in the Pots of Gartness. 80.
  • Later on the same trip. Dunno what he did, but he obviously deserved it. Fitz at Crainlarich. 80.
  • Just for a change, me and Fitz headed off to Ireland for a summer break. There was some legendary hitching - some bad some good. I suspect the bad caused us to catch the train.
  • When we got there, we dossed in St.Stephens Green, before hitting the country out West and finding this great bivvy above Clifden in Connemara.
