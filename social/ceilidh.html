Sarah & Paul's Wedding Ceilidh

We had always planned to have a nice wee party to mark getting married and Sarah decided on a ceilidh. We checked out venues and settled on Cranham Village Hall and we were really fortunate to be able to book Mark Evans band, Diskan. When the 26th September came around, all that was left was to get everyone on the bus, fire up the band, figure out where Brian was and set off.

As soon as the beer and square slice came out, everyone cheered up enormously. And while some tried hiding outside, Fletch asked Ash if his dance lessons had paid off. A ceilidh can be a cross between country dancing and full contact Twister.

The man from Uley Brewing had said that one polybin would be enough! Fortunately we hadn't had too much before trying this. You can't escape all the tradition. It was a great night. Meanwhile, back at the polybin .......
