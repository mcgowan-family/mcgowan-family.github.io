Washington, DC, 2007

Visited Washington on work trip and took some of the folk around DC on the Sunday. It was quiet as usual, and we parked up by the Capitol.

We stopped in by the White House, but there were no white squirrels.

Headed across to Arlington and paid our respects to the great man. It was a beautiful day and we saw the changing of the honor guard.

Came back over and had lunch in the JFK Centre for the Performing Arts in Watergate. Great views from the roof top terrace.

Popped in to see Lincoln, wandered round the Vietnam memorial, spent some time with Jefferson and finished off with a bit of FDR. Can't believe some of the spongers you see claiming benefit.
