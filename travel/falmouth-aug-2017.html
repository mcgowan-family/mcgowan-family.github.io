Falmouth, August 2017

We found a nice little flat down in Falmouth for our Summer break and for Moss's birthday. Itw as at the next beach along from Falmouth itself at a place called Swanpool.

As you might expect, we spent most of the week on the beaches nearby. Round the coast from Swanpool is the lovely little beach at Maenporth, which had a really cool little cave system that cut back uner the road into the tidal pool behind. Full of crabs!!

Falmouth is a great spot. The National Maritime Museum is loads of fun. Next door was The Shack, where we all enjoyed an amazing crab dinner.

On the way home from Falmouth, we crossed the Fal on the Fal on King Harry's Ferry and made our way to the lovely little village and castle at St.Mawes.

Everyone agreed it had been a top week!.
