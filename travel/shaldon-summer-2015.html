Shaldon, Summer 2015

Back to Shaldon and straight onto Ness Beach where Bryher turned into a mermaid. That was strange enough, but not as strange as Patrick morphing into an octopus!!!

There's always so much to do, but if in doubt, have an ice cream. We didn't see this last year - and they caught one. Grandpa and Grandma came along.

On a wet day, we headed up to Exeter. Lots to see around the old cathedral and we went to the climbing centre. Bryher took to it straight away, as did Patrick. He really loved this chimney.

We made it to the Crab Shack this time (it was closed last year). And the kids feasted on crab.

We headed up onto Dartmoor on the last day and climbed up onto High Tor.
