' Paris 08

Paris, August 08

Our honeymoon was always going to be brief, but we were loving every minute.

We stayed in a cool little hotel in Montmarte. We pretty much walked all over the centre of the city. It's behind you!

Nice view, but we decided not to waste any time queuing up. Instead, we hopped on the bateau bus, and nipped down to the Ile de la Cite.
