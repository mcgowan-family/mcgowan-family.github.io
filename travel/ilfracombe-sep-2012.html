Ilfracombe - Summer 2012

We popped down to Devon for what we thought would probably be our last beach break of the summer. Sarah found a nice little cottage just to the east of Ilfracombe, as the van was in Hulin's having the valves done. It was a great choice, as a working farm with animal feeding time for the kids and its own private beach. The sand was just right for making sand castles :)

Sarah was in her happy place and Patrick and Bryher loved it.

I'm not entirely sure what Bryher was looking for in this rockpool, but it must have got a fright when she found it.

After all the excitement of the beach, we deaded in to Ilfracombe the next day. We both really liked the town around the harbour, which was a complete contrast to our last experience a lifetime ago at the Paradise Cafe :)

As is traditional, we treated the kids to some special ice creams. Not sure if Patrick really liked his.
