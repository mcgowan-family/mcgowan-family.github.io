Sardinia 05

Spent a week in Sardinia in October with Ali - lots of scenery, sea, wildlife and architecture. Flew with easyjet into Olbia in the north-east. Stayed in the lovely old town of Alghero. Weather was great at this time of year (mid 20s) and crowds were nowhere to be seen.

Drove down the coast road to Bosa. Wandered round the old town. Popped into the Sella & Mosca vineyard. Popped up to Stintino and Spinosa for some great snorkelling.

One of the more famous sites around Alghero are the Grotto di Nettuno. It's a great boat ride out with some odd rock formations, great cliffs and then you get to the caves!

Check out the neolithic Nuraghes. Very Asterix & Obelix, but 1500 BC, so pre-arch, wheel and steel. How they ever put these things up is a mystery. The best one is at Santu Antine.

And don't miss the Pisan style churches scattered across the north of the island.

Beyond Alghero, there are other great towns to visit, like Castelsardo, Tempio and Oristano.
