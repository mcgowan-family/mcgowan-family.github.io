Sardinia, October 2017

As something different, we decided to splurge and take everyone off for a villa holday to Sardinia.

We had taken quite a while to find the right place. But it worked out well, with a lovely pool, local wildlife, imported wildlife and great landscape all around.

Moss was lapping it up and enjoying every miniute. And Grandpa couldn't have been happier with his little mate.

We hired a van and spent a day seeing some of the local scenery. Lots of neolithic remains. These are the famous nuraghi.

Grandma was there too and everyone had a lovely time. This part of the coast of Sardinia has amazing beaches, this is La Impostu.

And of course it was Bryher's 8th birthday while we were there.

Sadly and all too soon it was over. I wonder if this is how the boys will travel next time we're here.
