Frigiliana 2007

Finally got over to see Caroline, John, Gerry and Maureen's places near Nerja in Southern Spain.

Frigiliana is a great little white village. More developed than some, but still retaining the traditional atmosphere.

It was great to spend some time there with the rest of the folks and a good opportunity for Sarah to meet the rest of the family.

Did loads of snorkelling in the marine park east of Nerja, checked out the caves and wandered round the foothills of the Alpujara.
