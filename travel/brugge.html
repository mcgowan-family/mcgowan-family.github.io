' Barcelona 07

Belgium, April 07

Most of this trip was about our taking part in the Tour of Flanders sportif.

After watching the Classic Ronde van Vlaanderen on the Sunday, we drove to Brugge.

Nice place Brugge. Lovely old centre surrounded by a canal and lots of fabulous medieval buildings. City gates and windmills on the ramparts. Pretty unique.

We were in town over the easter weekend and they really go in for it i a big way. More bunnies and chicks than you can point a delicious plain chocolate stick at.

I was feeling a bit rubbish on the Saurday night - combination of too much sun/beer and onset of a bug, so I crashed out in the hostel. Rest of the guys went out and had a meal, but everyone was a bit wiped, so no night clubs that night.

Next morning we were all heading off, but we had a near crisis as I couldn't find the car keys. It was Easter Monday, so getting a spare key was impossible. Took me ages to find the key. Fortunately Sarah had stashed it in a safe place. Phew, what a relief; thanks Sarah ;o)
