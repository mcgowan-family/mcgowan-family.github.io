April

Did the sportif of the Tour of Flanders over the Easter weekend. really excellent trip.

After a lairy weekend up in Newcastle, followed by Leo's 30th, I did the HONC! No pic's, but it was best conditions I can remember. If anything, it was a bit too dry. We were running slicks again - probably over-inflated at 60 psi - and those rutted fields have an effect after 100km!

March

Been too busy to keep this up to date of late, but getting out loads.

Most recent outing was with Steve Pierini on the Spring Polaris event , that took place down in the Borders. HQ was at the excellent Traquair House and the route ranged from there, across the hills in and around the mountain bike centres at Glen Tress and Innerleithen, though most of it was outside of those centres on marked trails, like the Southern Uplands Way. Michael came down to see the start on Saturday and by the end of that day we were camped up over near Selkirk! Final results are on the Polaris website.

Next event is Tour of Flanders this weekend!
