Tetons, summer 2001

Back in the summer of 2001, I did a couple of mountains in the Grand Tetons National Park with Bill Crumm and Nigel Dewdney. The picture above is of the Grand Teton from the high campsite on Mount Moran.

  • Looking down from the CMC route onto West Horn, East Horn and the Falling Ice Glacier
  • Nigel scrambling up an easier section of the CMC route
  • Me on the summit of Mount Moran. We summitted on 21 August.
  • From the high campsite, looking up to the Black Dyke and the line of the CMC route
  • Nigel with the West Horn and Jenny Lake behind him
  • Bill bringing Nigel up on the CMC
  • Nigel goes a bit mental on the porridge

    • Later that week, me and Bill climbed Grand Teton via the Exum Ridge. We summitted early on the 24th of August.
